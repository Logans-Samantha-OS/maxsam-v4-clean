## OVERVIEW

Tarrant County (Fort Worth area) holds monthly delinquent tax sales on the first Tuesday of each month, conducted by the Constable for Precinct 3. When properties sell for more than the tax obligation, excess proceeds are held in the court registry. The county provides a "Motion to Distribute Excess Funds" form through their Law Library.

## DEADLINE TO FILE CLAIM

**2 YEARS** (before the second anniversary of the sale date)

---

## STEP-BY-STEP CLAIM PROCESS

### Step 1: Get Tax Sale Results
- Email openrecords@tarrantcountytx.gov
- Specify the month and year of the tax sale
- Request information on excess proceeds held in court registry

### Step 2: Download Motion Form
- Visit tarrantcountytx.gov
- Go to Law Library section
- Download "Motion to Distribute Excess Funds" form

### Step 3: Prepare Your Motion
Include:
- Property information
- Sale date and cause number
- Your claim and relationship to property
- Amount requested

### Step 4: File Motion with Court
- Submit to court that ordered the sale
- Can file under original cause number
- Pay required filing fees

### Step 5: Serve All Parties
- Serve copy on all parties to underlying action
- Must serve at least **20 days before** hearing date
- Follow Rule 21a, Texas Rules of Civil Procedure

### Step 6: Attend Hearing
- Court determines distribution priority
- Bring all original documentation
- Court issues order directing payment

## DISTRIBUTION PRIORITY (Per Texas Tax Code)

Court orders payment in this order:
1. Tax sale purchaser (if sale voided)
2. Taxing units for subsequent taxes/penalties/interest
3. Lienholders for amounts due under liens
4. Taxing units for unpaid amounts in judgment
5. Former owners as interests may appear

---

## IMPORTANT RESTRICTIONS

1. **Post-Judgment Owners** - Cannot claim if acquired interest after judgment date
2. **No Interest/Costs** - Interest or costs may not be allowed
3. **Service Required** - Must properly serve all parties 20 days before hearing
4. **Appeals Possible** - Order directing payment is appealable

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Use Official Form** - Download from county website for best results
2. **Request Results Promptly** - Get tax sale information early
3. **Serve Properly** - Follow Rule 21a exactly
4. **Allow Hearing Time** - Schedule before 2-year deadline
5. **Bring Originals** - To court hearing for verification
