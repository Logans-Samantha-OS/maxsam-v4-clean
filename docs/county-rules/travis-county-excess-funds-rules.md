## OVERVIEW

Travis County (Austin area) handles excess funds through the District Clerk's Finance Division. When property is sold at a tax foreclosure sale for more than the total tax obligation, the overpayment is called "excess proceeds" and is deposited with the Finance Division. Former owners are notified and have 2 years to file a claim.

## NOTIFICATION PROCESS

**Automatic Notification:** Upon receipt of excess proceeds greater than $25, a certified letter is sent to the former owner within 31 days.

---

## DEADLINE TO FILE CLAIM

**2 YEARS** from the property sale date.

Per Property Tax Code 34.021: If no claimant establishes entitlement within two years, the clerk's office distributes funds to each taxing unit that participated in the sale.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP CLAIM PROCESS

### Step 1: Request Excess Funds Listing
- Email dcfinance@traviscountytx.gov
- Note: Applicable fees may apply
- Get exact amount and sale date

### Step 2: Gather Required Documentation
- Deed showing ownership before judgment date
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of current address
- Relationship proof (if heir)

### Step 3: Prepare Court Petition
**Options:**
- Hire a private attorney, OR
- Visit Travis County Law Library at 1700 Guadalupe Street, 2nd Floor

### Step 4: File Petition
- Submit to Travis County District Court
- File under original cause number if available
- Pay required filing fees

### Step 5: Obtain Court Order
The signed court order MUST include:
- Recipient's name
- Recipient's address
- **Exact amount** of excess proceeds on deposit to be released

### Step 6: Submit Order for Payment
- Present signed court order to Finance Division
- Funds released per court order

## FEES

Travis County withholds the following fees:
- **5%** of total withdrawn (not to exceed $50) on uninvested funds in Registry account (Local Government Code 117.055)
- **10%** of total interest earned in any interest-bearing account (Local Government Code 117.054)

---

## COURT ORDER REQUIREMENTS

The court order **MUST** specify:
1. Recipient's legal name
2. Recipient's current address
3. **Exact dollar amount** of excess proceeds to release

**Missing any element will prevent processing.**

---

## IMPORTANT RESTRICTIONS

1. **Court Order Required** - No release without signed order from presiding judge
2. **Ownership Timing** - Must have owned before judgment date
3. **2-Year Deadline** - Strictly enforced
4. **Fees Apply** - Both for information requests and fund releases

---

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Email for Listing** - Get exact amount before filing
2. **Use Law Library** - Free self-help resources available
3. **Include Exact Amount** - Court order must match to the penny
4. **Account for Fees** - Factor in the 5% fee (max $50)
5. **File Early** - Allow time for court processing
