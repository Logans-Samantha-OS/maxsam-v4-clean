## OVERVIEW

Collin County (McKinney/Plano area) conducts Sheriff's Sales on the first Tuesday of each month at the County Courthouse. Properties are sold either as Tax Sales (for delinquent property taxes) or under Writ of Execution/Order of Sale (to satisfy judgments). When properties sell for more than owed, excess proceeds are held for eligible claimants.

## STEP-BY-STEP CLAIM PROCESS

### Step 1: Identify Excess Funds
- Contact Collin County District Clerk
- Request information on excess proceeds
- Verify sale date and amount available

### Step 2: Gather Required Documentation

**Essential Documents:**
- Transfer or assignment document (if applicable)
- Current deed
- Deed from when you owned the property
- Government-issued photo ID

### Step 3: Prepare Petition
- Draft petition for excess proceeds
- Include property details and your claim
- Reference original cause number

### Step 4: File with Court
- Submit to Collin County District Court
- File under original cause number
- Pay required filing fees

### Step 5: Serve All Parties
- Serve copy on all parties to underlying action
- Must serve at least 20 days before hearing
- Follow Texas Rules of Civil Procedure

### Step 6: Attend Hearing
- Present documentation and claim
- Court determines priority
- Receive order for distribution

## IMPORTANT RESTRICTIONS

1. **Post-Judgment Owners Cannot Claim**
   - If deed was recorded AFTER judgment date, you cannot claim
   - This applies even if you "owned" the property

2. **Strict Deadlines**
   - Tax foreclosure: 2 years
   - Missing deadline = funds go to taxing units

3. **Documentation Required**
   - Must attach proper documentation to petition
   - Include transfer documents, current deed, and ownership deed

---

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Verify Judgment Date** - Your deed must predate this
2. **Get All Deeds** - Both current and from time of ownership
3. **Document Chain of Title** - Shows your right to claim
4. **Act Before Deadline** - Allow time for processing
5. **Consider Assignment Rules** - If working with recovery company
